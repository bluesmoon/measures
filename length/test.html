Test shows that 1in in CSS is actually 13/16 of an inch on a 13" MBP screen, 27/32 of an inch on a 15" MBP screen and 23/32 of an inch on a 15" Thinkpad running Ubuntu Linux. In all cases it matched up with 96 pixels, which is expected for a 96dpi display. An actual inch measured 120 pixels on the 13" MBP, which is what you'd expect for a 120dpi display, but only 112 pixels on the 15", which confuses me and 138 pixels on the Thinkpad. On a 24" HP monitor, a logical inch is exactly 1 physical inch.

A note about the resolutions used. The 13" MBP was at 1280x800, the 15" MBP was at 1440x900, and the Thinkpad was at 1680x1050. The 24" HP was at 1920x1200. Go ahead and test it on your own screens.
